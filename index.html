About

Not just a meal.
A moment.

Otoro Supper Club is a premium seafood hospitality brand built around the theater of exceptional fish — most notably, the live bluefin tuna cutting experience. We turn high-quality seafood into a social event that feels educational, luxurious, and unforgettable.

Where most event catering is functional, Otoro is a focal point. Guests don't just eat — they witness the craft, engage with the chef, and walk away with a story worth telling.

The Experience

deliberate.

Live Bluefin Cutting

Watch as our chef breaks down a whole bluefin tuna tableside — a dramatic, precise ceremony that captivates every guest in the room and signals that what follows is something truly different.

Chef-Grade Presentation

From hand rolls assembled to order to meticulously arranged charcuterie and sashimi spreads, every plate is designed to look as exceptional as it tastes. The table becomes part of the décor.

Premium Hospitality

We handle sourcing, cold chain, staffing, setup, and service so hosts can be fully present with their guests. Luxury buyers need confidence — our team delivers it from first inquiry through final cleanup.

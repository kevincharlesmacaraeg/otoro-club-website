An Experience That Becomes
the Story of the Summer.

Your members don't just watch — they gather around, ask questions, and taste cuts they've never encountered outside of Tokyo. Then they sit down to a world-class omakase built entirely from the fish they just witnessed.

About Otoro Club

Built for the World's Most
Discerning Palates.

Otoro Club was founded with a singular vision: to bring the ancient Japanese art of maguro kaitai — the ceremonial breaking of a whole tuna — to the most exclusive private settings in North America.

Our zero-waste sourcing model means every part of the fish is honored. The live ceremony feeds your guests. The remaining cuts are transformed into our curated DTC subscription boxes, delivered directly to attendees' homes — extending the experience well beyond the event itself.

Based in La Jolla, California, we source directly from Pacific fisheries with full chain-of-custody documentation. Every fish arrives within 48 hours of harvest.
